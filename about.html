Who We Are

Smart Cycling Hub is a comprehensive digital platform designed to empower cyclists of all levels with the tools, knowledge, and resources they need to enjoy cycling safely and confidently. Whether you are a daily commuter navigating city streets, a weekend warrior tackling mountain trails, or a road cycling enthusiast training for your next event, Smart Cycling Hub provides personalised guidance tailored to your needs.

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with expert cycling knowledge. The AI-powered Bike Finder analyses your budget, preferred terrain, and experience level to recommend the perfect bicycle from real bike shops and manufacturers. The Route Insight tool integrates with mapping services to calculate cycling distances in kilometres and estimated travel times in hours, minutes, and seconds, helping you plan every ride with precision.

Beyond technology, we believe in building a cycling community grounded in safety and education. Our curated collection of 20 cycling tips covers everything from helmet safety and tyre maintenance to nutrition and group riding etiquette. The Maintenance Guide provides detailed care instructions for five different bike types — city, electric, mountain, road, and gravel — ensuring your bicycle stays in peak condition regardless of how and where you ride.

Smart Cycling Hub was created as an academic project to demonstrate how modern web technologies can serve real-world cycling communities. We are committed to making cycling more accessible, enjoyable, and safe for everyone.
